SCP-3305: The Father, The Son, and The Holy Toast

by Captain Kirby

SCP-3305 is a collection of five trees in the ██████████ Forest with slices of white bread stapled to them. All attempts to remove the bread from the trees have failed.

News

October 7, 2018

The international branches of the SCP community have teamed up to bring you a collection of translated SCPs from around the world! New articles are posted daily, and you can view them at the International Branches Hub!

August 17, 2018

The SCP-4000 Contest is over! Be sure to check out all the entries! Series V is now open!

July 12, 2018

SCPDeclassified, a subreddit devoted to literary analyses and plot explanations of SCP articles and concepts, recently reached 10,000 subscribers. Congratulations to them, and we look forward to reading their future declassifications!

June 20, 2018

The 4000 Contest is live! In honor of the site's 10th anniversary, the contest's theme is HISTORY! Here's to an epic contest and another ten years of SCP!

May 21, 2018

The Portuguese Branch is the newest member of the International SCP community! We wish them the best of luck and great success!
